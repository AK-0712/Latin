Noun case chart

Latin Noun Case English Purpose of the Noun Case Purpose
Nominative Subject; Predicate Nominative Controls the action in the sentence; paired with a linking verb
Genitive Possession Shows ownership of something
Dative Indirect Object Someone or something affected by the action of the verb but is not the primary object of the verb
Accusative Direct Object; Object of Preposition A person or thing that is the recipient of the action of the verb or preposition
Ablative Variety (Ablative Absolute, Ablative of Means, etc.); Object of Prepositions A person or thing that follows a preposition, or is part of a phrase
Vocative Direct Address A person or thing that is verbally addressed in a sentence
