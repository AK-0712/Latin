Latin Writing Assignment

Write three sentences in Latin giving details about the case or asking questions about the case. One sentence must contain a noun-adjective pair. One sentence must contain a semi-deponent verb. One sentence must the name of one of the suspects, witnesses, or informants.

45–35 points

The Latin writing assignment includes three complete sentences in Latin.

 

The sentences:

  • use a variety of vocabulary that fits the context of the mystery
  • consistently use correct grammar, syntax, and mechanics (i.e., punctuation and spelling)
  • are creative and demonstrate originality

 

Grammar:

Student uses all of the following grammatical constructs correctly:

  • correct form and usage of the noun-adjective pair
  • correct form and usage of the semi-deponent verb
  • correct form and usage of Latin names

34–25 points

The Latin writing assignment includes three complete sentences in Latin.

 

The sentences:

  • use a variety of vocabulary that mostly fits the context of the mystery
  • include grammatical structures with errors that generally do not impede comprehension, and the mechanics (i.e., punctuation and spelling) are usually correct
  • are somewhat creative and demonstrate originality

 

Grammar:

Student uses most of the following grammatical constructs correctly:

  • correct form and usage of the noun-adjective pair
  • correct form and usage of the semi-deponent verb
  • correct form and usage of Latin names

24–0 points

The Latin writing assignment does not include three sentences in Latin or/includes three incomplete sentences in Latin.

 

The sentences:

  • do not use appropriate vocabulary
  • do not use correct grammar, syntax, and/or mechanics (i.e., punctuation and spelling)
  • do not demonstrate originality

 

Grammar:

Student uses few or none of the following grammatical constructs correctly:

  • correct form and usage of the noun-adjective pair
  • correct form and usage of the semi-deponent verb
  • correct form and usage of Latin names

10 points

5 points

0 points
