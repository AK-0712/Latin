Attention! Attention! This just in!

A theft has occurred at the Roman Forum in the Temple of Vesta!

Oh no! It’s the Palladium statue…only one of the most sacred, precious, and powerful objects the world has ever known. Legend has it that a city which possesses the statue is divinely protected from invasion and captivity. Should the Palladium leave the city’s walls, destruction, darkness, and utter despair await its residents.

The Palladium must immediately be returned to the Temple of Vesta, or the destiny of Rome is…doomed!

Sleuth, we’re counting on you to figure out who stole the Palladium statue. Witnesses saw a shadowy figure running out of the temple with a statue. Whodunit? Why was it stolen? Piece together the clues in this course and solve this mystery for us. Please hurry. Catch our thief. Find the Palladium...before it's too late.
